Sai Prema - A Center for Children with Special Needs

Sai Prema, A center for children with special needs (Intellectual disabled, Down's syndrome, Slow learners and Children with ASD) was started with a spirit to reach the maximum children in need of special care. The motto of the institute is to provide a model program for children with special needs in participation of their parents and families with specially developed innovative practices and multidisciplinary therapeutic interventions.

Our Mission

To provide comprehensive support and specialized education to children with special needs, while empowering their families and creating inclusive communities that celebrate diversity.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their abilities, has access to quality education, therapeutic support, and opportunities to reach their full potential in a nurturing environment.

Our Inspiration

Smt. Bikkina Jhansi Lakshmi

Principal, Sai Prema SMART Special School

Our inspiration stems from the remarkable journey of Smt. Bikkina Jhansi Lakshmi, a mother to a Intellectual disabled child and a professional Special Educator with 38 years of experience in training children with special needs.

Having completed her PG Diploma in management of voluntary welfare organizations from Andhra University, she was deeply moved by the struggles of many deprived parents of children with special needs. This empathy and understanding inspired her to establish a comprehensive training and therapeutic center.

Her dedication to providing specialized care, combined with her personal experience as a parent, drives our mission to create inclusive opportunities for every child to reach their full potential.
